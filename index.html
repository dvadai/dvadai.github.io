Read our story

Our story began at a house party in 2014. We both liked each other very much but none of us wanted to be in a relationship in the close future. We started talking and all we remember from that night is we went home with the feeling of meeting someone special. A few days later Steve asked Dia out to go on a date but she got scared of what if she does not like him by the time they get the starter and then she has to spend hours with him, so she suggested an alternative option: going cycling. An hour long cycle date turned into a full day. This is also one of the reasons how we found out we could have a hobby together. Thousands of kilometers and a lot of sore ass pains later we decided that we would like to tandem forever together.


The venue

The restaurant is called: Kisfaludy-ház Étterem It was originally a wine making press-house and beloned to a famous Hungarian poet, Kisfaludy Sándor. It was built in the 18th century in a Baroque style. The house has been beautifully renovated and has a fantastic view upon a hill overlooking Lake Balaton.

Lake Balaton

The "Hungarian Sea" is the people's name for the 50-mile long lake with silky green-yellow water. Lake Balaton is a real Hungarian treasure, offering a long shoreline dotted with family resorts on the southern side and welcoming vineyards to the north.

But do not worry, just remember to drink water and eat because these drinks have a very high alcohol concentration.
